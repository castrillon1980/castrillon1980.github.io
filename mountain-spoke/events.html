Cycle the Continental Divide

OCBC Cycle Continental Divide 2018 will fill our May race calendar. A weekend of 2 events at 2 mountain biking venue! The downhill championship will be battled on the Super D descent of Bozeman Park and 24hrs later the cross country championship at Mountain Bike Park at Bozeman, Montana.

Date:
18 May 2018, Friday (8AM – 12noon) – Downhill 19 July 2016, Saturday (6.30AM – 1PM) – Cross Country (XCO) Notes : All participants in the event MUST hold a SCF National Race Licence (2a or 2b) or a UCI International Race Licence All participants in the event will requires to hold the requisite insurance coverage as per stated in SCF National Race Licence guidelines Insurance may be purchased through SCF, which cover you for all races for entire year.
