Franklin Breaks Record

Franklin is an independent city having its beginnings in the 1830s as a railroad stop along the Blackwater River. During this era, the river was used to transport goods to and from Albemarle Sound. The climate in this area is characterized by hot, humid summers and generally mild to cool winters, Franklin has a humid subtropical climate
